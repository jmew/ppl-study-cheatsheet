Testing Supplement Cheatsheet

During the PAR knowledge test the FAA hands you the Airman Knowledge Testing Supplement — a booklet of figures and legends. Everything below is printed in that booklet, so you can look it up instead of memorizing it. Each topic links to its spot on this site, and every figure or legend links to that page of the FAA PDF.
