ATC & Communications

Non-towered communications

CTAF
The published self-announce frequency at a non-towered field (or when the tower is closed).
UNICOM
A private advisory station — can give an airport advisory (winds, favored runway, known traffic).
MULTICOM
Always 122.9 — the self-announce frequency when a field has no tower, FSS, or UNICOM.
Inbound with no tower, FSS, or UNICOM

Self-announce your position and intentions on MULTICOM 122.9 starting 10 miles out.

Unsure of the winds or active runway?

Call on the CTAF and request an airport advisory before joining the pattern.

Satellite airports

Departing a satellite airport inside Class C or D: contact ATC as soon as you take off.
